Lobster

6 servings

3 hours

Ingredients

  • 100g pacetra
  • Salt and peper
  • 3 large eggs
  • 50g parmesan/li>

Directions

Gather the ingredients.

Finely chop the 100g pancetta, having first removed any rind. Finely grate 50g pecorino cheese and 50g parmesan and mix them together.

Add 1 tsp salt to the boiling water, add 350g spaghetti and when the water comes back to the boil, cook at a constant simmer, covered, for 10 minutes or until al dente (just cooked).

Flip the lobster back over, slowly and carefully pull up the lobster meat in one piece. The meat should still be attached at the bottom of the tail.

Then, you'll remove the mixture from the heat and cool it to room temperature.

Enjoy!
